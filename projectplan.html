Healthy Living Hub - Wireframe Plan

Site Name

Healthy Living Hub

Description

A comprehensive platform for healthy living, featuring pages dedicated to healthy recipes, workout routines, and mental wellness practices. Users can explore content that encourages a balanced lifestyle, with interactive features and a sign-up for a weekly newsletter.

Pages & Features

  • Healthy Recipes - A collection of nutritious, easy-to-make recipes with tips for meal planning and healthy eating.
  • Workout Routines - Tailored workouts for all fitness levels, including strength training, cardio, and flexibility exercises.
  • Mental Wellness - Resources and tips for improving mental health with an interactive button to add self-care activities.
  • Newsletter Sign-Up - Allows users to sign up for weekly health tips and motivational content.
