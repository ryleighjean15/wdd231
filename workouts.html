Workout Routines

Full Body Workout

This full-body workout routine is designed to target all major muscle groups. Perform each exercise for 3 sets of 12 reps.

  • Push-ups
  • Squats
  • Bent-over Rows
  • Plank
  • Burpees

Cardio Workout

Get your heart pumping with this high-intensity cardio workout. Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, followed by 15 seconds of rest.

  • Jumping Jacks
  • High Knees
  • Mountain Climbers
  • Butt Kicks
  • Jump Squats

Core Workout

Strengthen your core with these exercises. Perform each exercise for 3 sets of 15 reps.

  • Crunches
  • Russian Twists
  • Leg Raises
  • Bicycle Crunches
  • Flutter Kicks
